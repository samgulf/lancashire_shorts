Lancashire Shorts

Welcome to Lancashire Shorts. Short audio plays, conceived, written and produced in Lancashire. The plays cover a wide variety of genres, (not horror) and are intended to provide light and humorous entertainment. The plays are free to listen to, but we hope you might consider making a small donation, via Ko-Fi, to a special Lancashire charity we support. We hope you do enjoy them. We are keen to add plenty more as finances allow! Phil and the team
